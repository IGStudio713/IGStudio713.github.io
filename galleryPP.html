1. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how Gallery Slimmer handles user data, directly or through third-party services, in compliance with applicable laws such as GDPR and CCPA.

2. Data Collection

The app itself does not collect any personal data. However, integrated third-party SDKs may collect user-related data for functionality such as advertising, analytics, and consent management.

3. Types of Data Collected

  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
  • Advertising identifiers (e.g., IDFA)
  • App usage statistics
  • Approximate location (via IP address)
  • Crash logs and diagnostics
  • Transaction identifiers (from in-app purchases)

4. Purposes of Data Usage

Collected data may be used for the following purposes:

  • Showing personalized or contextual advertisements
  • Analyzing user engagement and app performance
  • Managing user consent and preferences
  • Supporting in-app subscriptions and transactions

5. Third-Party Services

Gallery Slimmer integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob – Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics – Privacy Policy
  • Facebook Analytics – Privacy Policy
  • Google UMP SDK – Consent Management Guide

These services operate under their own policies and may collect data based on user consent and regional requirements.

6. In-App Purchases

Gallery Slimmer provides in-app purchases and subscriptions. All payments are securely processed by Apple. The app does not store or access any payment data. Learn more at Apple's Privacy Policy.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent user data. Data may be shared with third-party SDK providers only as needed for the purposes listed above, and only if consent is given when required.

8. User Consent

The app uses the Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) SDK to obtain user consent where required. Consent choices are stored and respected according to applicable laws (e.g., GDPR, CCPA).

9. User Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Request access to data collected about you
  • Withdraw consent or opt-out of data collection
  • Request deletion of your data (if applicable)

10. Data Storage & Security

All data collected through third-party SDKs is stored on their respective platforms (Google, Facebook) using industry-standard encryption and access controls.

11. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe this has happened, please contact us.

12. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with a revised date.
