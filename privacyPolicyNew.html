Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This application collects only the data necessary to provide and improve the gameplay experience, analytics, and monetization.

1. Data We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Anonymous gameplay statistics (levels completed, session length, etc.)
  • Crash reports (only if you choose to send them)
  • Advertising identifiers (e.g., IDFA, GAID)
  • Device and usage data (model, OS version, language, etc.)
  • Randomly generated numeric user ID
  • Optional: username and email for leaderboards or support

2. Analytics

We use Unity Analytics to collect aggregated, anonymized gameplay data such as retention, session length, and in-game behavior. This helps us improve the game's features and balance. No personal data is linked to this analysis.

Learn more: Unity Privacy Policy

3. Advertising and Tracking

This app uses AppLovin MAX for ad mediation, which integrates multiple ad networks (such as AdMob, Unity Ads, IronSource, etc.). These networks may use identifiers like IDFA/GAID, cookies, and device information to serve personalized or contextual ads based on your preferences and legal requirements.

Users in the EU are shown a GDPR consent dialog, and iOS users are prompted for ATT tracking permission (App Tracking Transparency).

Learn more: AppLovin Privacy Policy

4. Leaderboards & Cloud Saves

If the game includes leaderboards or cloud saves, your game score, nickname, and optionally your email may be stored on a secure server. Email is used only to link your progress or to contact you regarding issues you initiated.

5. Crash Reports

If the app crashes, you may be prompted to send a report. This includes technical information about the crash (not personal data). If you enter your email voluntarily, we may contact you to investigate and resolve the issue. Providing your email is optional.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. All analytics and advertising are either anonymous or shown with age-appropriate settings and parental consent where required.

7. Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA)

You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your data, where applicable. Since most data collected is anonymized or aggregated, these rights may not apply in practice.

8. Data Sharing

We may share anonymized data with our service providers for analytics and advertising purposes. We do not sell your personal data.
