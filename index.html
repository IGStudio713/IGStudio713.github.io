Our Apps

Easy Piano Learning

Music has the power to stimulate the brain, and Easy Piano Learning takes advantage of this by turning the process of learning piano into an enjoyable game. This app is designed for users who want to improve their memory and cognitive functions while mastering piano pieces. Through the use of gamification techniques, users can learn to play songs step by step in a fun and rewarding way. Each session is crafted to challenge the mind and enhance memory retention, making the app ideal for both children and adults who wish to enhance their mental acuity while enjoying music.

Drawing Club

Art is one of the purest forms of self-expression, and Drawing Club provides an intuitive platform for anyone to learn how to draw. From absolute beginners to more experienced artists, this app offers guided lessons, tutorials, and creative exercises that foster artistic growth. With a focus on simplicity and progression, users can develop their skills at their own pace. The app covers various drawing techniques, helping users build confidence and improve their artistic capabilities with each session.
