Privacy Policy for Easy Piano Learning

This privacy policy explains how the Easy Piano Learning app (hereinafter referred to as "the App"), developed by Elmar Rasulzade, handles and protects user data.

1. Data Collection

The Easy Piano Learning app does not collect or store any user data. We do not use advertising identifiers, nor do we collect any personal or anonymous data about user behavior.

2. Use of Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party libraries or services that may collect user data. All functions of the App are implemented without the need for processing user data.

3. Age Restrictions

The App is available for use by people of all ages, including children. We aim to provide a safe and user-friendly platform for learning piano.

4. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to make changes to this privacy policy. However, we are not required to notify users of any updates.
