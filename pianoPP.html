Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how the Easy Piano Learning app (hereinafter referred to as "the App"), handles and protects user data.

1. Data Collection

The Easy Piano Learning app collects information about users' age groups anonymously. This information is solely used to help the developers understand the age groups of users and to improve the quality of the service. All collected information is completely anonymous, and we do not store any other personal information.

2. Use of Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party libraries or services that may collect user data. All functions of the App are implemented without the need for processing user data.

3. Age Restrictions

The App is available for use by people of all ages, including children. We aim to provide a safe and user-friendly platform for learning piano.

4. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to make changes to this privacy policy. However, we are not required to notify users of any updates.
